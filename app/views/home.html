Mission

ITNSunCoast, founded in 2010, is a nonprofit transportation service serving Sarasota and Manatee Counties. Our mission is help people age 60+ and adults with vision challenges maintain their independence and live joyful and meaningful lives by connecting them to each other and the community.

Regeneron and ITNAmerica

ITN{{affiliate}} is part of the nationwide network of ITNAmerica affiliates. ITNAmerica is proud to recognize Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. as a corporate sponsor committed to helping raise awareness of the challenges associated with vision impairment, low vision, senior mobility issues and the shortage of locally-available transportation options for seniors. As the National Pharmaceutical Eye-care Services Sponsor for ITNAmerica and our affiliate communities, Regeneron Pharmaceuticals is proud to support ITN's mission to promote safe senior mobility.
