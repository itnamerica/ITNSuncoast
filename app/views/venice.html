Venice Calendar & Activities

Venice
July 4, 2018 to July 31, 2018

**Christmas in July Craft Show- Great deals from local vendors so you can finally get a jump on this year’s Christmas shopping. Free Event!

**Christmas in July Downtown- Check out the sales downtown, sign up for a raffle, meet with Santa or take a trolley ride

**Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone.

**Fourth of July Fireworks- Go out to the beach to see the fireworks display! Free Event!

**Galleria Matinee- $6 Movies! If you prefer to spend those hot summer days indoors, let us know what movie you’d like to see.

Habitat ReStore & Lunch- Get in some shopping at the ReStore where they have everything from clothing to furniture and then go to lunch at a TBA location.

**Historic Spanish Point- Come see the gardens at a special Let’s Go Places price of $10 for first time riders.

Myakka Wildlife Airboat Tour- See our local flora and fauna in a unique way. Tours are $15.

**Senior Friendship Center– Live music and dancing with the Let’s Do It Band. Concerts are $7 for non-members. The Sprouts Program provides FREE produce to seniors.

**Venice Farmer’s Market- An eclectic mix of local produce, plants, crafts and artwork, ready-made delicacies and more!

**Venice Museum- A collection pertaining to area history, housed in the historic Triangle Inn. FREE!
