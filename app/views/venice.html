Venice Calendar & Activities

Venice
October 2018

Notes (**Indicates a Free or Low Cost Event or other discount):

**Bentley’s Homemade Ice Cream- Few things are better than ice cream on a hot day and we’re giving you two opportunities.

Bertha Palmer Historical Lecture- Kate Holmes has been volunteer of the year for her presentations on Bertha Palmer.

**Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone.

**Elite Health- Sugar Blues addresses how to eat less sugar in a world where it appears in everything. The inspirational speaker is Terrie Hoops a cancer survivor and Ironman competitor. Call Tonya at 941-234-0587 to RSVP!

**Galleria Matinee- $6 Movies! If you prefer to spend those hot summer days indoors, let us know what movie you’d like to see. Octoberfest Venice Style- Check out downtown’s spin on a fall classic.

**Senior Friendship Center– Halloween Party will have a costume contest with prizes and live music. Entry is $5. Sun Fiesta- One of the area’s largest festivals featuring food, music and more! FREE EVENT!

**Venice Farmer’s Market- An eclectic mix of local produce, plants, crafts and artwork, ready-made delicacies and more!

**Venice Museum- A collection pertaining to area history, housed in the historic Triangle Inn. FREE!

Venice Theatre-Suds bubbles over with nostalgia, comedy, and more than 30 songs of the 1960s, including favorites like “Johnny Angel” and “It’s My Party.” . South Pacific is a crowd favorite set on an island paradise. Call 488-1115 for tickets!
