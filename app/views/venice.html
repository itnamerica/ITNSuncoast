Venice Calendar & Activities

Venice
August 2018

**Bentley’s Homemade Ice Cream- Few things are better than ice cream on a hot summer day and we’re giving you two opportunities to do so.

**Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone.

**Galleria Matinee- $6 Movies! If you prefer to spend those hot summer days indoors, let us know what movie you’d like to see.

Habitat ReStore & Lunch- Get in some shopping at the ReStore where they have everything from clothing to furniture and then go to lunch at a TBA location.

**Lunch & Learn- Medicare: What You Don’t Know Can Hurt You. Free event, call 548-4433 to RSVP!

**Senior Friendship Center– The Annual Luau will have live music & dancing while the Disaster Preparedness Seminar will help you prepare for the upcoming hurricane season. Both are free events!

**Venice Farmer’s Market- An eclectic mix of local produce, plants, crafts and artwork, ready-made delicacies and more!

**Venice Museum- A collection pertaining to area history, housed in the historic Triangle Inn. FREE!

Venice Theatre- VT has several offerings this month including some one of a kind cabaret performances by local favorites like Kristofer Geddie and Michelle Kasonofsky. They’re also doing the Sondheim favorite Into The Woods. Call 488-1115 for tickets!

Venice Wine Walk Downtown- Check in to get a $10 wristband and stroll historic downtown Venice while enjoying specials at various restaurants and shops.
