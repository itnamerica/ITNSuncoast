Venice Calendar & Activities

Venice
November 2018

Notes (**Indicates a Free or Low Cost Event or other discount):

**Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone.

**Elite Health- Lunch & Learn about Holiday Weight Gain or come to the Latin Spice Salsa Event and try some Latin food and dance. Call Tonya at 941-234-0587 to RSVP!

**Galleria Matinee- $6 Movies! If you prefer to spend those hot summer days indoors, let us know what movie you’d like to see.

**Historic Lecture on the Military Institute- Larry Humes will be going over 126 years of history for the Kentucky Military Institute. This event is free of charge!

JoyRides!- A new activity we’re offering at ITN, JoyRides are a 1-2 hour excursion tailored to your tastes in music, theme or location. Call 364-6530 for more information, JoyRides are $40.

Madgrigal Dinner- A madrigal dinner and show supporting the Venice High School Chorus. Tickets are $25, call 218-3779 for reservations.

**Senior Friendship Center– Bring a dish to share at the Thanksgiving Potluck or see a show at the Inner Voices event. Call 556-3263 to register for the event!

**Venice Museum- A collection pertaining to area history, housed in the historic Triangle Inn. FREE!

**VPAC- Venice Concert Band is honoring our soldiers and veterans with their Honor America concert. Tickets are $12 cash at the door.

**Elite Health- Lunch & Learn about Holiday Weight Gain or come to the Latin Spice Salsa Event and try some Latin food and dance. Call Tonya at 941-234-0587 to RSVP!

Venice Theatre- George Grove and Rick Dougherty, formerly of the Kingston Trio, and Jerry Siggins, lead singer of The Diamonds, sing the Great American Folksong Book. Call 488-1115 for tickets!
