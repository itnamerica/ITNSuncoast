Venice Calendar & Activities

Venice
May 7, 2018 to June 1, 2018

**Caspersen Beach - Come enjoy the natural beauty Venice has to offer. Take a stroll down the beach or take a dip in the Gulf if you’re feeling daring.

**Galleria Matinee - $6 Movies! If you prefer to spend those hot summer days indoors, let us know what movie you’d like to see.

**Historic Spanish Point - Come see the gardens at a special Let’s Go Places price of $10 for first time riders.

**Memorial Day Ceremony – Sponsored by VFW Post 8118, and free of charge, a ceremony honoring our fallen heroes.

Myakka Wildlife Airboat or Tram Tour - See our local flora and fauna in a unique way. Tours are $15.

**Senior Friendship Center – Live music and dancing with the Upbeat Gang. Concerts are $7 for non-members. The Sprouts Program provides FREE produce to seniors and the FREE Memorial Day Party will have music, dancing and fun while honoring our military.

**Venice Farmer’s Market - An eclectic mix of local produce, plants, crafts and artwork, ready-made delicacies and more! **Venice Museum - a collection pertaining to area history, housed in the historic Triangle Inn. FREE!

Venice Theatre – The 23rd Annual Loveland Show is one of the feel good shows of the year! Done in partnership with the Loveland Center it gives a chance for adults with intellectual and developmental challenges grow and learn through theatre study. Please call the box office at (941)488-1115 to purchase tickets.
