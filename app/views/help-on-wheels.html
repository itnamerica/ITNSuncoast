Help on Wheels

As a proud affiliate of ITNAmerica, ITNGateway provides dignified transportation for seniors, adults with disabilities, and the visually impaired in St. Charles County and seniors and the visually impaired in designated zip codes in St. Louis County.

We help families stay connected and friendships endure. ITNGateway helps adult children minimize absenteeism from work, businesses remain profitable, and enables seniors to begin a new chapter in their lives that replaces dependence and isolation with independence and mobility.

ITNGateway provides over 900 rides per month in St. Charles County. The average cost to ITN of providing each ride is $12 (in addition to the fee paid by the rider). Our newest fundraising campaign, Help On Wheels, aims to cover the cost of these rides, and will allow ITN to continue to grow and serve the community.

With a $12 per month recurring donation, you can support one ride per month to a St. Charles County or to a St. Louis County senior citizen, or disabled resident.

Our goal is to collect 600 recurring donations of $12 per month each.

It takes only a couple of minutes to sign up, and we will send you a Help on Wheels mug (In addition to a donation receipt for tax purposes).

Please consider making this small tax-free recurring donation to ITN, to keep dignified transportation available in St. Charles County and St. Louis County.
