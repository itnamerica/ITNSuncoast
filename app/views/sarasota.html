Sarasota Calendar & Activities

Sarasota
September 2018

Notes (**Indicates a Free or Low Cost Event or other discount):

**Brant’s Used Books- A bibliophile’s dream with over 5000 sq ft and 150,000 titles of books!

Clyde Butcher Gallery- Meet special guest Paul Marcellini, a Florida wildlife photographer and see his work.

**Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone. Bring your calendar for a 15% discount!

FST- Twelve unique improv games of Comedy Lottery will offer a one of a kind performance that will have you rolling in the aisles. Tickets are $15, call the box office at 366-9000!

**Ringling College- Be inspired by the work of the talented Ringling College faculty in the Basch Visual Arts Center. **Ringling Museum– A fantastic collection of European art, it’s FREE on Mondays!

Sarasota Players- Annie Get Your Gun is an Irving Berlin classic with favorite tunes such as “There’s No Business Like Show Business”. Call 365-2494 for tickets!

Senior Friendship Center- Skip’s Dixie Mix will be performing live and The Center for Brain Health is offering FREE Brain Health Screenings. Please call 556-9900 for a screening appointment.

Screenings- There are several classic movie screenings around Sarasota this month. Sabrina, an Audrey Hepburn classic, will be shown at the Opera House for $10. The Royal Shakespeare Company’s Macbeth will be at the Historic Asolo Theatre along with An American in Paris. These two movies are $18 each.

**UTC Mall- With over 100 stores and restaurants it’s a window shopper’s dream!
