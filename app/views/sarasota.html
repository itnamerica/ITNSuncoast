Sarasota Calendar & Activities

Sarasota
October 2018

Notes (**Indicates a Free or Low Cost Event or other discount):

**Brant’s Used Books- A bibliophile’s dream with over 5000 sq ft and 150,000 titles of books!

**Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone. Bring your calendar for a 15% discount!

**Downtown Craft Festival- Explore a variety of artisan’s booths featuring everything from jewelry to photography to paintings.

**First Watch- The perfect place for brunch with a 10% Let’s Go Places discount!

FST- Unchained Melodies celebrates the swinging harmonies of the 50’s and 60’s with favorites like “Blue Moon” and “Save the Last Dance”. Call the box office at 366-9000 for tickets!

Historic Trolley Tour- This 2 hour tour is narrated by historian Sue Blue. Call 941-364-9076 to reserve a space. Tickets $30.

**Phillippi Farmhouse Market- This seasonal farmer’s market is back with local produce and crafts!

**Ringling College- Opening night reception for the student art show, see the early works of some of tomorrow’s greats.

**Ringling Museum– A fantastic collection of European art, it’s FREE on Mondays!

**Senior Friendship Center- Nancy and Keith will be performing live for your listening and dancing pleasure.

**Towles Court Art Walk- A chance to meet artists and enjoy live music in our own art local artist colony!

**UTC Mall- With over 100 stores and restaurants it’s a window shopper’s dream!
