Sarasota Calendar & Activities

Sarasota
July 2, 2018 to July 31, 2018

**Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone. Bring your calendar for a 15% discount!

**Cuban Ballet School Showcase- See the students showcase their skills. Ticket are $25, call the box office at 328-1300.

**Downtown Sarasota– Stroll through Downtown Sarasota, and check out some of their unique stores and restaurants.

**Fireworks at Nathan Benderson Park- Free Event with live music and fireworks. Bring something to sit on if you’d like.

**Florida Studio Theatre- Music of the Night: The Swingaroos jazz up Broadway favorites. Call 366-9000 for tickets!

**Gone With the Wind Screening- The classic film is going to be screened at the Sarasota Opera House. Tickets are $10.

**Ringling Museum– A fantastic collection of European art, it’s FREE on Mondays! Call 360-7399 for tickets!

**Ringling College Galleries- See illustrations from Octavio Perez or new work by the Trustee Scholars amongst others.

Sarasota Players- Tops from Flops is a revue of some of the most treasured Broadway tunes from some of its biggest flops. Call 365-2494 for tickets!

**Senior Friendship Center- Enjoy singing? Come and try your hand at karaoke! Free Admission!

**UTC Mall- With over 100 stores and restaurants it’s a window shopper’s dream!

**Vintage & Collectibles Fair- Books, antiques & more. $3 Entry!
