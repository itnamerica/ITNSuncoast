Sarasota Calendar & Activities

Sarasota
May 7, 2018 to June 1, 2018

**Downtown Farmer’s Market - An eclectic mix of local produce, plants, ready-made delicacies and more!

**Historic Spanish Point- See the gardens at a special Let’s Go Places price of $10 for first time riders.

**Lido Pool- Cool off, swim laps or just soak up the rays at the pool. Admission is just $2!

Marie Selby Gardens- A tropical oasis with unparalleled horticulture displays right on the bay. Tickets are $20.

**Parkway Cinema- If you prefer to spend those hot summer days indoors, let us know what movie you’d like to see.

**Ringling College- The works of nationally known illustrator, Octavio Perez, will be on display in the Thompson Gallery.

**Ringling Museum– A fantastic collection of European art and it’s FREE on Mondays!

Sarasota Orchestra Pops Concert– A range of popular hits in an outdoor setting with special guests and a fireworks display. Call 893- 6300 to get your tickets ahead of time as they often sell out!

**Turtle Beach- Come enjoy the natural beauty Sarasota has to offer. Take a stroll down the beach or take a dip in the Gulf if you’re feeling daring.

**Venice Downtown– Take a stroll down picturesque Venice Ave, and check out some of their unique stores and restaurants
