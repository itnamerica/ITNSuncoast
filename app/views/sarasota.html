Sarasota Calendar & Activities

Sarasota
August 2018

**Brant’s Used Books- A bibliophile’s dream with over 5000 sq ft and 150,000 titles of books!

**Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone. Bring your calendar for a 15% discount! Dog Days Theatre- The Turn of the Screw is an adaptation of the chilling Henry James ghost story. Call 351-8000 for tickets.

**Downtown Sarasota– Stroll through Downtown Sarasota, and check out some of their unique stores and restaurants.

**Florida Studio Theatre- Gilbert & Sullivan Unplugged is a twist on songs from The Mikado, HMS Pinafore and more. Call 366-9000 for tickets!

**Orioles- See the Gulf Coast League games for free! On the 8th they will be offering a Wellness Fair with free screenings and more during the game.

**Ringling Museum– A fantastic collection of European art, it’s FREE on Mondays! Sarasota Players- See the classic play On Golden Pond or something new with Before Steepletop. Call 365-2494 for tickets! Urbanite Theatre- Wakey, Wakey is a spirited portrait of humanity filled with gratitude and humor. Call 321-1397 for tickets **UTC Mall- With over 100 stores and restaurants it’s a window shopper’s dream!

**Van Wezel: Friday Fest- See the country band Rebel Heart outside the Van Wezel. This is a free event!
