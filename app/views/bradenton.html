Bradenton Calendar & Activities

Bradenton
November 2018

Notes (**Indicates a Free or Low Cost Event or other discount):

**AMC Matinee- If you prefer to spend those hot summer days indoors, let us know what movie you’d like to see.

**Blues Festival Concert- Free event featuring the Memphis Rub Band and Bridget Kelly Band! Bring a blanket or chair to sit on. **Culvers- From burgers to frozen custard they have something for everyone. Bring your calendar for a 15% discount!

**Manatee Village Historical Park- A collection of historic buildings and artifacts which tell the story of pioneer life in Florida. Free admission, but donations are welcome.

**Ellenton Outlets- Great for window shopping and offers some of the best deals in the area!

**Mixon Fruit Farm & Wildlife Rescue- Take a tram tour through the groves, gardens & wildlife rescue. Tram tickets are $10.

**Palmetto Historical Park- Take a tour through some of the oldest buildings in the area. Free admission, but donations are welcome.

**Red Barn Flea Market- Enjoy the open air farmer’s market & hundreds of booths for finding hidden treasures.

South Florida Museum- Visit the manatees, take in a show at the planetarium, or wander the exhibits. Tickets are $17.

Symphony on the Sand- See the symphony performed on Anna Maria Island. Ticket range from $20-$125. Call the box office at 941- 795-2370 for tickets and be sure to bring a blanket or chair.

**Village of the Arts- A mix of galleries & restaurants in a charming neighborhood. Take a stroll, grab a bite & be inspired!
