Bradenton Calendar & Activities

Bradenton
June 4, 2018 to June 30, 2018

**Ellenton Outlets- Great for window shopping and offers some of the best deals in the area!

**Hurricane Preparedness Seminar- Find out what you should know about the upcoming hurricane season, or if you’re already prepped browse some books or movies at the library. Free Event!

**Palmetto Historical Park- Take a guided tour through some of the oldest buildings in the area including Palmetto’s first free standing post office. Free admission, but donations are welcome.

Manatee Performing Arts Center- Tony n’ Tina’s Wedding is an immersive theatre event based on an Italian American wedding, full of warm and intrusive stereotypes exaggerated for comic effect. Call 748-5875 to purchase tickets.

**Marauder’s Baseball Game- See a home game at Lecom Park where the Marauders take on the Threshers. Tickets range between $6-$10.

**Mixon Fruit Farm & Wildlife Rescue- Take a tram tour through the groves with stops in the gardens and wildlife rescue. Grab some lunch at the café. Tram tickets are $10.

**Red Barn Flea Market- Enjoy the open air farmer’s market & hundreds of booths for finding hidden treasures.

South Florida Museum- Visit the manatees, take in a star show at the planetarium, or wander the exhibits. Tickets are $17.

**Village of the Arts- An eclectic mix of galleries, restaurants and services in a charming neighborhood of colorful bungalows. Take a stroll, grab a bite and be inspired!
