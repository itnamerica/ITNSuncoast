Board of Directors

Suzy Prymmer

Chair

Suzy Prymmer, Executive Director of Savannah Grand of Sarasota, was born and raised in Youngstown Ohio. Suzy has a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Youngstown State University, with a minor in advertising. She is also Sarasota Core Trained and is a licensed Assisted Living Administrator in the State of Alabama. She has served on many boards and is looking forward to serving as Chairwoman of the ITNSuncoast Board.

Suzy is married to Lee Prymmer, an engineer, for over 16 years and has a wonderful blended family of five children, Sarah, Jason, Elizabeth, Justin and Valerie, who have given them three handsome, high-spirited grandsons, William, Conner and Abram. In addition to loving her family, she also enjoys boating, fishing and knitting. Suzy’s passion for seniors is exceptional. Suzy has been in senior housing industry for 15 years and held several positions as executive director in Alabama and Florida.

Linda Davis

Secretary

Linda has been a seasonal resident of Sarasota for several years. During this period she has been involved increasingly in community activities. Linda is an ITNSunCoast volunteer driver, the newsletter and social chair for her neighborhood association, and coordinates colleague retiree events and communication.

She has over 30 years of experience as an educator, teaching biology and being the gifted education liaison in her school district. Linda is an avid golfer and journey woman glass fusing artist. Linda has a particular interest in fundraising, communications and volunteer recruitment.

Donald Deibert

Treasurer

Don Deibert moved to Lakewood Ranch in December 2015. He had lived in North Carolina for the previous 28 years. After his parents passed away, Don moved to Florida to be near his brother and family.

Don has served on many Boards of Directors and has served as Treasurer. He currently manages a small family private foundation. He also coordinates the Road to Recovery Program in Buncombe County N.C., which transports cancer patients to their treatment appointments. He was Treasurer for a county-wide book reading organization in Buncombe County. He has been involved in many volunteer opportunities in North Carolina and now in Florida.

Don started out and continues as a volunteer driver for ITNSunCoast. He offered to be on the Board of Directors and now has agreed to become the Treasurer. He is committed to our community and wants to give back. He feels that ITN Is a wonderful opportunity for seniors who can no longer drive. He lives full time in Florida.

Dianna Holmes Gray

Board Member

Spending most of her life in the Long-Term Care, Assisted Living and Home Care industry, Dianna Holmes Gray is a Master’s Level Social Worker, Licensed Nursing Home Administrator and National Certified Care Manager. Dianna received a B.A. from Wright State University in Dayton, Ohio and a Master’s Degree in Social Work Administration with a concentration in Alzheimer’s and Dementia Care from The Ohio State University.

Dianna has focused her career on opening and leading newly built Senior Living Communities including Independent Living, Assisted Living and Long-Term/Short-Term Nursing and Rehabilitation Centers in Ohio, Indiana and Florida.

Born and raised in Ohio, Dianna moved to Florida from Fort Wayne, Indiana in January 2015. Formerly an Executive Director for an Assisted Living Community in Sarasota, Dianna is a Social Worker for Tidewell Hospice.

Dianna enjoys serving others, especially Seniors, in the beautiful sunshine state of Florida and lives in Venice with her husband Duane. They enjoy spending time at the beach and savoring the beauty of this wonderful Suncoast area.

Christina Speir

Board Member

Christina Speir is Vice President of Professional Relations at Tidewell Hospice in Sarasota, Florida. She has a Master’s Degree in Business Administration and a Bachelor’s Degree in Communication Sciences and Disorders. Christina has a strong background in healthcare, management and volunteer background. She has worked for Tidewell Hospice for 10 years and has served as chairs on committees for the American Cancer Society and the Humane Society.
