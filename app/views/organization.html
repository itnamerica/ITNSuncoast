Staff

Arthur Lerman

Executive Director

Arthur Lerman serves as the Executive Director and Chair of the Board of Directors of ITNSunCoast. He has over twenty years of experience in providing services to seniors and managing non-profit organizations. He has been particularly successful in growing both non-profit organizations and for-profit businesses and in developing innovative programs. Arthur has managed a home health agency, assisted living facilities and nursing homes.

He was the executive director of non-profit organizations that provide services to individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities. He has consulted with states and counties across the country on their long term care and behavioral health programs. Arthur received a B.A from Clark University in Worcester, MA and a Masters Degree from the University of Pennsylvania.

As an elected official, Arthur served two terms as a member of the State of Maine House of Representatives. He was a member of the Appropriations and Taxation Committees, founded and chaired the Developmental Disabilities Caucus, was House Lead on Joint Select Committee on Property Tax Reform, and served as the House Chair of Study Commission Regarding Livable Wages. Arthur introduced numerous bills related to health care and environmental protection and was even able to get some legislation enacted.

Since moving to the Sarasota area, Arthur has served on the Sarasota County Seniors Advisory Council and been on the Board of Directors of the Area Agency on Aging of Southwest Florida. He also was the facilitator of the HealthCare Workgroup, an affiliate of SCOPE and participates in the Stakeholders in Aging Consortium.
